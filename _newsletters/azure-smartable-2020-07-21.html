1. Microsoft migrates ‘Minecraft’ from Amazon Web Services to its own Azure — According to CNBC, Mojang plans to migrate Minecraft away from Amazon Web Services (AWS) and over to Azure. Since 2014, Mojang has used AWS to offer Minecraft Realms. The $8 per month subscription service allows players to create private play spaces for their friends and them without having to deal with the challenges of setting up and hosting a private server.
